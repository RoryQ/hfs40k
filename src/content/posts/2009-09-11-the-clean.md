[![](/hfs40k/images/covers/2009-09-11-the-clean.webp)](/hfs40k/images/covers/2009-09-11-the-clean.webp)The Clean are a hugely influential lo-fi/post-punk/indie pop band from New Zealand. I don't actually know how well known they are outside of New Zealand, but like the Verlaines, famous bands from other parts of the world namedrop them to be cool.

[![](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010094353im_/http://intothemusic.ca/images/uploads/covers/Clean_-_Anthology.jpg)](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010094353/http://intothemusic.ca/images/uploads/covers/Clean_-_Anthology.jpg)

*Anthology* (2003)[VBR]

Their early lo-fi indie stuff and their most popular.

*Unknown Country 1996* [256 m4a]

Something from their more recent, mature period. Probably not as appealing as their early work, it's without that lo-fi jangle-pop charm, but it's their best in my opinion. Available in FLAC if anyone particularly wants it.
