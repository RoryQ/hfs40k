[![](/hfs40k/images/covers/2009-12-06-wayne-shorter-alegría-flac.webp)](/hfs40k/images/covers/2009-12-06-wayne-shorter-alegría-flac.webp)Excellent, excellent live album from a true jazz legend and genius both in composition and improvisation. Included are arrangements running the gamut from retakes of his own work such as "Orbits" from his days with Miles Davis and "Angola" from his The Soothsayer album (which I posted here a few months ago) to reworkings of old folk songs ("12th Century Carol") and modern day classical music by the likes of Heitor Villa-Lobos, perhaps the most prominent Brazilian composer of the 20th century.  
  
With a wide variety of instruments (cello, oboe, flute, bass clarinet, bassoon, horn, tuba, etc.) reinforcing the strong compositions, this is a tour de force of modern jazz. Highly recommended.
