The final disc of the compilation, the songs making up the piece under the same name. The most enjoyable thing on there imo, angular no-wave/post rock kind of.

I was listening to that Arvo Part I posted a little while back, more people should really try that out. It's choral of course, but I love it and I don't usually like choral stuff. It's strange and minimalist, with quick crescendos and disappearances (forgive my lack of knowledge in the way of classical music) and lovely background arrangements. Kind of like what Sunn O))) attempted to do on some of the tracks on Monoliths and Dimensions but didn't succeed as well as Part does there (of course. Also, Monoliths and Dimensions is one of my favourite albums of 2009 so far), so just give it a try
