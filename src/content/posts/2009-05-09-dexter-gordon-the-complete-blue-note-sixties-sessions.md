The Complete Blue Note Sixties Sessions is a six-disc set featuring all of Dexter Gordon's '60s recordings for the label in chronological order. Such albums as Dexter Calling and Go! were recorded during these years, and they are presented in their entirety, as are two complete sessions previously unavailable on cd and several unreleased alternate takes.
