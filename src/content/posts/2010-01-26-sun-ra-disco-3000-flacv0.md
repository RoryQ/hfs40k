Sun Ra here again with what is left of his Arkestra (4 members.) As a quartet: Sun Ra on synthesizers/organs/keys/piano, (the amazing) John Gilmore and tenor saxophone, Michael Ray on trumpet, and Luqman Ali on drums. Occasionally there is special guest June Tyson who sings. Unlike the original 1979 LP release, this is the 2007 remastered full concert in Milan in January of 1978. For some retarded reason, people ignored this. It's pretty much everything a fan of his more avant-garde works would want: a mix of Sun Ra's famous drones, an occasional drum machine, and some nice hard bop. I could listen to Sun Ra wank on a keyboard for several hours, but that's just me. Space is the place.
