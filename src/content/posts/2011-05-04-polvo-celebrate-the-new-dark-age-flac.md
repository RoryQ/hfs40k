That's Polvo's music. Math rock, they call it. They were one of the originators. The fractured, complicated melodies. The ever-evolving time signatures, etc. Except in Polvo's case, it's not wank. These are SONGS (hi, other math rock bands). They're PLEASANT TO LISTEN TO. They have lyrics and such. But they're also thoroughly well-composed, which is why Polvo are a joy to listen to.

"Fractured (Like Chandeliers)" is fucking amazing. Listen to it. Jesus Christ it's like the guitars are dueling dragons wrapping around themselves as they ascend into the heavens of indie rawk. Yeah dawg.
