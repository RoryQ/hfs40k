Built to Spill's "Perfect From Now On" has slowly become one of my favorite albums thanks in large part to Martsch's twisting, complex compositions and emotive and, importantly, NON-SHOWOFFY guitar solos. That all comes to a head in this album, the aptly titled "Live". The 20-minute cover of Neil Young's "Cortez the Killer" is more than worth the price of admission (in this case, nothing; lucky you).
