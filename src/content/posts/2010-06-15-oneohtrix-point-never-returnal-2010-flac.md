Quite honestly one of our most anticipated albums of the year so far, and a genuine contender for the best of 2010, 'Returnal' is the fourth LP from the Boston/New York based synth dreamer, Oneohtrix Point Never.  
  
This is an essential purchase for lovers of Fever Ray, Popol Vuh, Klaus Schulze, Autechre, Aphex Twin or Jan Hammer. Brilliant.  
  
P.S. First track has very little to do on how the whole album sounds.
