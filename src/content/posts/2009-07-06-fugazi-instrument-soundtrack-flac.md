Probably the best "demos and outtakes" album that was ever produced. Instead of having completely worthless recordings left on bits of tape that the band's record label pushes out to take advantage of the most dedicated fans, this collection of odds 'n' ends shows off Fugazi jamming, which is a pretty damn good thing to hear considering the instrumental skill that these guys have, especially their amazing rhythm section. One listen to "Lusty Scripps" will prove exactly what I mean.
