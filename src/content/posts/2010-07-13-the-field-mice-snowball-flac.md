With multiple 5+ minute tracks and 2 over 6 minutes, I was wondering whether I had stumbled upon some horrible mixture of progressive rock and twee pop. But alas, thank the heavens, this was not to be my fate. Instead, I jumped headlong into some dreamy, summery, virginal, twee'd out bliss. Ohhhh, yeaaahhhh. Melodies that will give you cavities if you listen long enough. This re-release includes a bunch of their most excellent singles. "Couldn't Feel Safer" is the life of a jamtime.
