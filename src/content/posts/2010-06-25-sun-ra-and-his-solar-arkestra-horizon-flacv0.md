They wandered around Cairo and famous Egyptian ruins (see: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5azChH6Z7QA) and played a couple of shows funded by the Egyptian Ministry of Culture. Now Sun Ra had always been obsessed with ancient Egypt as well as the FUTURE, so what else does he do but perform one of his most intense shows there (Dec 17th of 1971) as well as solo on a Minimoog! The moog creater gave one of his first prototypes to Sun Ra, and Sun Ra experimented with it to create really awesome water-splash noises as well as twinkly-zappy sounds that really don't make any fucking sense unless you actually listen to this record. Leave it up to some random self-funded British label to re-package, re-master, and re-released a lot of Sun Ra records Evidence didn't release already (thank you...Art Yard. I love you.)
