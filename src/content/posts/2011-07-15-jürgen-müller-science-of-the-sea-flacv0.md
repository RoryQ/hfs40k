While he had been fascinated by the water since childhood, Müller hadn't felt it as a muse until he conducted research on the North Sea. Inspired by "the mystery and romance of the great seas," Müller borrowed synths from friends and put his limited keyboard skills to work. Think of Brian Eno's Discreet Music or the minimalist efforts of the Berlin School era of early German electronic music. Very pleasant and warm. Also uses heavy amounts of looping arpeggios like Emeralds on their most recent album.
