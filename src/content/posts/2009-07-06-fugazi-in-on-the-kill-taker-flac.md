From the first few moments of "Facet Squared," as Joe Lally's bass and Brendan Canty's drums come rumbling in, Fugazi snatches you up and makes everything else seem inconsequential for that gripping 42 minutes and 10 seconds. I'm not going to do one of my awesome reviews, which I'm sure will disappoint all of you greatly, but I.B. scores came out today and I'm a little exhausted. Rest assured, it's a damn good album by my favorite rock band.
