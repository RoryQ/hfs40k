David Francolini, Christian Hayes, and Laurence O'Keefe were once in Levitation--a group that made it their *raison* d'*être* to play very noisy psychedelic guitar music, and slowly go round the bend. Twenty Twenty Sound, is the much-vaunted experimentalism from their new band--which can almost be described as sonic terrorism. Dark Star is devilishly effective, with "I Am The Sun" and "Graceadelica" being the best tracks here, although each song is worthy of your attention.
