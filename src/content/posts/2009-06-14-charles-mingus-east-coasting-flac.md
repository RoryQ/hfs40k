Interesting session that was overshadowed by the contemporaneous release of "Tijuana Moods", a more highly acclaimed album. That isn't to say that this album is unworthy, because it is Mingus, after all. This session is noteworthy because of the presence of a young Bill Evans, who in just a few years would go onto his ensemble work with Miles Davis that would start him on his rapid ascent to legendary status.  
  
In other words, just listen.
