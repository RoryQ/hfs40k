Such a beauty of an album, with the trumpet flourishes, riddims, and of course the excellent vocals. The lyrics touch on Marcus Garvey, Jah, and the history of people of African origin, among other things. Decidedly less dubby than The Congos but still as amazing, make sure you at least listen to this once.
