I excitedly posted this on my blog only to find that nobody really gave a shit (woe is me)

Nature and Organisation is Michael Cashmore who writes the 'best' music for Current 93. Current 93 rulz 'cause Tibet's lyrics are amazing, Nature and Organisation has little guest spots from Tibet, Douglas P. and Rose McDowell, but I find the music most affecting. I don't actually pay any notice to the lyrics when I'm listening to it! There's a cool connection to the Wicker Man in *Wicker Man Song* which is *Willow's Song* which is my favourite folk song. 5/5!!
