It's probably one of the most powerful vocal performances by a unique vocalist I have heard since Tim Buckley's album Starsailor (also on this blog in FLAC.) Catherine has a deeper voice, almost like Nico, but WAAY more powerful and engaging. Leader of the backing band Alpes is Patrice Moullet, who provides most of the genius guitar moments on this album, and even performing with his own invented instruments. With Moullet's beautiful arrangements that create a psychedelic texture, and assistance in songwriting (he co-wrote every song except "Roc alpin" which is his own), this album is a true psych folk masterpiece that is still completely ignored by everyone except RYM users.
