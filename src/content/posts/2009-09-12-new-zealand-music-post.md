**Genre:** Indie/Jangle-Pop

**Sounds Like:** The Velvet Underground, The Clean

**Download:** [Here](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010094702/http://lix.in/-506e4f)

Posted before. Part of the *Dunedin Sound*. The whole jangle-pop Velvet Underground worship known as the *Dunedin Sound* was popular during the 80s, this is the period where New Zealand music was/is most acclaimed. The Verlaines are the smart band with the Dunedin Sound, their name is a reference the Paul Verlaine, and they crammed everything from Schubert to Verlaine's homosexual relationship with Rimbaud into their single, 'Death and the Maiden'. Downes is now an intellectual lecturing at Otago University, [see](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010094702/http://www.otago.ac.nz/music/staff/graemedownes.html).

The start of the uglier side of the 80s in NZ music.
