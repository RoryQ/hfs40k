I would not recommend this to people who are just learning about the French duo (search "The Dance of the Moon and the Sun"). NSB is a guy and girl French Folk duo who are pretty much the top of the psychedelic drone/folk scene and limit copies of their work but personalize them very much with their own packaging and drawings.
