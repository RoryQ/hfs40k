Now, I may be biased since I have a sick fetish for all things David Lynch/Angelo Badalamenti/Twin Peaks, but this is my favorite dream pop album ever.  I've read reviews that focused on how Cruise is basically acting out a role prepared for her by Lynch, filling that void of a picturesque 50's starlet whispering over a darkened room. While I'd agree that the concept is Lynch's, I'd disagree that the fact that he conceptualized this negatively affects it in any way. The songs may be a *little* cheesy now, but some of us like a little cheese.   
  
The highlights of the album are obviously Falling and Rockin' Back Into My Heart, but if you've seen Twin Peaks, you already know and love these songs. As far as other great tracks go, my favorite is probably I Float Alone, followed by Into the Night. Both tracks are just so smooth and calm. I guess the whole album is, really, but these two stand out for me. Also, Into the Night has the award for "Song that Scared the Shit out of me the Fastest." When you listen to it, you'll know what I mean.
