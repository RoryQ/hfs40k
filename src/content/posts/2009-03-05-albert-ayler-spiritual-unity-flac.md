"*Spiritual Unity* makes everything about jazz modern history completely irrelevant. The rhythm is very free and abstract, and Ayler's child-like melodies are a paradox with its own brutality; it is a new revival of timbre and tonalities. In fact, it is probably more of a retroactive statement than it is an avant-garde assertion. The entire albums is very emotional, free and deeply rooted in spirituality such that it recalls the original intentions of jazz music: human expression. Entirely timeless and definitely one of the greatest recordings in the past century." -[youngprayer](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010164847/http://rateyourmusic.com/%7Eyoungprayer)
