I'm lyfm and I'll be posting all kinds of stuff here, mostly jazz and early 90s hip-hop. Introductions aside, this is a fantastic album. William Parker, whose "Double Sunrise Over Neptune" is already linked here, gets a great band together and goes through a few songs by Curtis Mayfield, keeping their soul intact but really exploring them with the bandmates throughout. The vocal work by Leena Conquest is also top-notch. Give it a listen if you're into jazz, soul or funk. It's more accessible than a lot of jazz and the live atmosphere is well maintained with shoutouts, introductions and just the right amount of audience noise.
