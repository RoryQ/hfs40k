Zs is a pretty cool avant-garde rock/jazz quartet from Brooklyn, New York (how surprising) Their most recent effort is the double album New Slaves, with two guitarists/noisers, a drummer, and a saxophonist who create noise jams that make Keiji Haino jealous. "Concert Black" begins with a "click clack click clack" and all of a sudden it's Link on LSD running through Forest Temple thinking he is being chased by a clock and when he appears out the other side what other than a bunch of HANDCLAPS and metul percussions and frenzied noise-funk that is deliciously titled "Acres of Skin". The next two tracks are made by the two noisers, who you guessed it, like noise. And they're actually quite good too. "Masonry" was written by the drummer who mellows out the much louder part of the album with sweeping vibes and xylophones. It's quiet, and then "click, click, click" NOISE JAM NOISE NOISE JAM NOISE NOISE JAM that is the title track that is 20 minutes of noise-faggotry and Can-like drum jam. The final two tracks have saxophonist member wanking on low notes while a quiet ambiance floats around, and the last track sounds like walking through the street of a busy afternoon at a market somewhere. Pretty freaking awesome if I dare say so myself.
