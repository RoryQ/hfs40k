Goldie's Timeless is what all drum'n'bass strives to be. It's got the funk and it doesn't let go of it for a whole 2 CDs. The beats are far more interesting and beefy than your usual "kick, snare, kick-snare". What puts the 'bass' in drum'n'bass has little to do with distortion or LFO, but liquid sub-frequencies and rubber bass licks. It's all coated with a fitting selection of pads and R'n'B/soul/jazz samples that guide the flow rather than distract you from it. And a cherry on top.
