[![](/holyfuckingshit40000/images/covers/2009-05-28-eric-dolphy-at-the-five-spot-vol-1-flac.webp)](/holyfuckingshit40000/images/covers/2009-05-28-eric-dolphy-at-the-five-spot-vol-1-flac.webp)Booker Little was an amazingly gifted trumpeter who died much too early: at the age of 23 from kidney failure. Eric Dolphy was a gifted multi-instrumentalist who met a similarly tragic fate. Together, these two made some of the best post-bop/avant-garde jazz that I've heard to date. This is a live album taken from their stint at the titular Five Spot Cafe. Highly recommended.
