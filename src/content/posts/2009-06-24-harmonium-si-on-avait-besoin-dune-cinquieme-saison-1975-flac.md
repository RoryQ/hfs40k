The album title translates to "If we needed a fifth season".  
The songs themselves represent seasons:  

* "Vert" = spring
* "Dixie" = summer
* "Despuis l'automne" = (after)fall
* "En pleine face" = winter
* "Histoires sans paroles" = a fifth season
