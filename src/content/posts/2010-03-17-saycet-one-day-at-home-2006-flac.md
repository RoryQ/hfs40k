sayCeT is an electronic act from France that makes quite gorgeous and really beautiful IDM/ambient music. One Day at Home offers really calming tunes, tender keys and sweet synth sounds accompanied with bitcrushed and glitched beats. Check it out if you dig Boards of Canada like electronic music or múm kind of atmospheres.
