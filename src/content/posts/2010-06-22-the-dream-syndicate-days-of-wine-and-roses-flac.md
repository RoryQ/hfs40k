It's been a while since I've listened to the Velvets and I never really favored them, so I couldn't exactly say whether or not those comparisons ring true. What I can say, however, is that I thoroughly enjoyed this album, and it sounds dated, but in a good way. Like, you can imagine you and your collegiate friends cruising from record store to beach to record store in the summer all along to the sounds of the Dream Syndicate. Great stuff. Again, recommended by [Recklessnin](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010173302/http://www.lastfm.es/user/ReCklessnin).
