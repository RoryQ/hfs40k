It took awhile to fully appreciate these, but deep down their among his most well composed music. The sheer amount of layers and complexity inside the music is wonderful. The symphonies are filled both with dread and horror as well as love and ecstacy. His music is primal much like fear, love, anger, and sex. If you listen to his other works, I think you'll find alot more ambience and less structure. In this album however his music is much more disciplined.  
  
 To truly fall in love with these symphonies, I highly recommend also getting his dvd "live at the kitchen". Hearing these works recorded live and watching the different rows of guitars play their parts really makes the music pop. These symphonies took on a whole different life after watching that dvd, but either way you cannot go wrong with these symphonies. This is Glenn Branca at his most refined and structured, all while never losing his chaotic essence. True it doesn't have strong melodies or catchy hooks, and I admit it took awhile to love his music. But let yourself sink into the music, set aside any distractions, and in time I think everyone can learn to appreciate and understand the passion here. His music is raw, primal, emotional, haunting, chaotic, joyous, spiritual, and sexually charged all at once.
