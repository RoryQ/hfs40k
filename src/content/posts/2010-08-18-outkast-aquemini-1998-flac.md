"Aquemini is Outkast at the peak of their creativity, the group kept on pushing their music forward and released one hell of a classic with their 3rd album. Southernplaya was the musical masterpiece, ATLiens was the lyrical masterpiece but with Aquemini they have a perfect balance between both elements and with a heavy dose of originality on top of it. Many tried to imitate but still over the years, there isn't a single thing that sounds like Aquemini. Well even the album doesn't have a "sound", it goes in all sorts of directions but it never sacrifices cohesiveness. It's southern hip hop that doesn't sound like any other southern hip hop but at the same time it's southern as fuck. The production is just nuts, hip hop never sounded like this and they took the genre to where it never been before, tracks like "Rosa Parks" and "Synthesizer" are simply unique and forever will be... incredible, incredible beats with the group slowly starting to produce their own stuff too. And these guys weren't slacking lyrically too, one hell of a performance from Andre 3000 and Big Boi, so much skills and it's never a problem for them to adapt to the many production styles. Aquemini is one of the most essential hip hop LP's out there, has aged incredibly well and still remains one of the freshest things you'll ever hear."
