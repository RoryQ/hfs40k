This is a concept album I guess, since all the songs make up the one big experience. Stylistically it's very cinematic with string instruments all over it with IDM sound backing it up. It would do a fine soundtrack to some sad war movie, with some sci-fi elements, that's what it brings to my mind anyway. Really worth checking out, the atmosphere is kinda haunting at times, which I dig.
