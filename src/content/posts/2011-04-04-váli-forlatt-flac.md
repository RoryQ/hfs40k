**Introduction:** Beginning with the first artist in the order they appear on [Whom The Moon A Nightsong Sings](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010145952/http://holyfuckingshit40000.blogspot.com/2011/04/thank-you-tolkkii.html)(HFS:40000 Post) is Váli. [Forlatt](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010145952/http://rateyourmusic.com/release/album/vali/forlatt/) is a very melancholic neofolk/dark folk album, much like most music I'll be posting at first since it's all artists from that compilation. This album is all acoustic instruments with the only vocals being on the track *Lengsel,* sparingly used*.* Sometimes referred to as a demo, Forlatt is Váli's only release.

**Part of Last.fm's Description:** "Vàli’s solo work involves acoustic guitar pieces, occasionally accompanied by instruments such as flute and cello. He has released one album, Forlatt (2004), which is now currently out of print. A five album-contract has been signed with the label Auerbach Tonträger."
