As per the usual, I'm listening to the album I'm writing up as I write it up. The words, however, are not coming. I'm simply in awe. There's only three instruments present: guitar, violin, and drums. If you're a Dirty Three fan like I am, you'd recognize that combination and the amazing, heart-wrenching pyrotechnics that it can bring forth. So with that in mind, don't be surprised when this album transports you...somewhere else. Takes you on....

....

....an odyssey.

Also, unlike in many albums, the vocals (present on a few tracks, sparingly) don't detract. In fact, they make it more soulful. Ulmer's voice sounds a little Hendrixy, and it just sounds like he's so in the zone he just has to blurt something out. Great shit man. Yeah man.
