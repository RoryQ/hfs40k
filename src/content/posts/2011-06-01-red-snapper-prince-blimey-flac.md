If you miss the psych-jazz genius bands of the Seventies like Colosseum, If and Nucleus, then you'll love this record. It's like stepping back into London 1971. The only complaint I have about the record is that I think the drums are tuned a little too tightly. They could use a more looser sound to match the loose grooves, but I'm just quibbling. There's some great playing going on this one.
