This is the birth of the abstract hip hop label anticon. It features nasal Idaho hippie Doseone, the gingersnap from Maine (Sole), another guy from Maine (Alias) and Slug, who is famous for being the rapper half of the duo Atmosphere. Ant, the producer of Atmosphere makes the beat for one track ("The Scarecrow Speaks"). This album is really a stream-of-conscious effort that doesn't really rap about one particular topic. It's not ridiculous like what anticon would turn into (this is an actual hip hop album.) It's probably my favorite whitey hip hop album, and it's pretty good.
