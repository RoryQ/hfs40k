Terence lays it out, an overview of his ideas. He talks up so much in symbiotic overview of prehistory and how what's happening in this modern Archaic Revival and where that's leading us. All of this is told to some of the most totally tripped out jazzed rocking trance and it all just combines into something you can groove to.
  
  
Mckenna knows how to Rave, giving us insight into shamanism and the plane world that surrounds us everyday. He speaks of the human mind our body's connections to nature with community, sexuality, and intelligence that leads to our eventual jump to hyperspace.
  
  
This album trip is a cosmic journey taking you up! Terence explains the DMT experience. DMT is a chemical inside the human brain that is classified as an illegal substance, so under the current law every human being is in possession of a class one drug.
  
  
Terence explains humans are the result of billions and billions of years, that our bodies are the cutting edge of organistic transformation of matter in this cosmos. The most novel thing known to humanity in existence is what sets behind your eyes. We are nature's most brilliant experiment thus far. We are capable of using our energy to create temples, art, bombs to use our energy against an enemy. But things are changing, the world is about to transform itself, supernatural reality is pulling us forward into collective transformation.
  
  
The psychedelic shaman is the one who can receive messages from the future to change the present, Someone who has dissolved the boundaries between the past, present and future. We are part of the planetary destiny. What psychedelics mean is a celebration of life of love and reemergence of a community not of consumerism.
