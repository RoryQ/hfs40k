Jimi Hendrix mixed with Ornette Coleman playing funky blues-influenced guitar in a jazz setting. None of that you need to imagine, though. Just listen to it on this album. And while we're at it, why don't we just toss in the real Ornette Coleman? This album is fucking amazing. His method of guitar playing had definite influences on Sonny Sharrock. If you want to hear exploratory jazz guitar that isn't just another Grant Green soundalike, hit this up. Thank me later.

I can't stop listening to this.
