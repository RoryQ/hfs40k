Iron Lung is one of my favorite artists in the power violence / hardcore punk genre. Their latest album "Sexless // No Sex" has been virtually the only album from the style I've played over the past year. Sporadic pacing keeps you interested the whole way and if you're a fan of the genre it's worth your while to check it out.  
 [Sexless // No Sex](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010094411/http://lix.in/-5fe503) (2007)  
  
[![](/holyfuckingshit40000/images/35e33fbe7f50818f1646cf53607c39af.webp)](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010094411/http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_X0HDv9LMCK8/SsDaNhlHZ-I/AAAAAAAAAEs/Z8tpa5aJjq0/s1600-h/2208615.jpg)Pig Heart transplant is a project headed by Jon Kortland of Iron Lung, and a complete departure from the aforementioned band. Total fucking Swans worship with a modern spin. The album is oppressive and ugly, and is like if early Wolf Eyes starting covering Swans songs. Their band page refers them to "Ugly Colossal Sound" so you can get an idea. Even though it is reminiscent of Swans, it's its own beast and totally worth a listen.
