Here is Mingus' sextet on March 18th, 1964 playing at Cornell University with none other than Eric Dolphy a few months before his death. Personnel include Johnny Coles on trumpet, Jaki Byard on piano, Cliff Jordan on tenor sax, Dannie Richmond on drums, Eric Dolphy on alto sax/bass clarinet/flute and of course Mingus on bass (and the occasional "vocals".) Has a lot of great virtuosic playing including "ATFW You", a wonderful piano solo by Byard and Mingus' bass solo cover of Duke Ellington's "Sophisticated Lady". Dolphy's clarinet solo on "Meditations" may be my favorite of his. It's a long album but well worth at least one listen.
