Posting some Finnish prog to big up my homeland, but this is essential listening. It's an instrumental concept album about a magpie called Bialoipokku. The English name for this album was "B The Magpie". I've decided to keep the track titles in the original Finnish because I don't think the English translations are accurate. Anyway, it's simply amazing all the way through. Track 6, "Sekoilu Seestyy" ("The Madness Subsides" on the English translation) is not only a stunningly beautiful piece of music but will be recognizable to DJ Shadow fans as that great sample that drives what's arguably his best track, "Midnight In A Perfect World".

The numbering on the filenames is because this is the second half of a CD that contained two Pohjola albums, but this is Harakka Bialoipokku in it's entirety. If there's demand, I have two more Pohjola albums to share.
