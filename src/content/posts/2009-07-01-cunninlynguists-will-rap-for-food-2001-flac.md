Spotlight time  
  
Fun-loving in nature, both as far as beats and flows go. Wordplay is the key here, whatever sounds good and rocks the house is hot to trot. Kno's beats are class, as soon as Lynguistics starts then the door is kicked down for the rest of Will Rap For Food. Beat mongers will love it, so should those who like funky southern rapping that is reminiscent of earlier Outkast. CunninLynguists come with their own style of self-made emceeing all while breaking cliches
