The back of this CD's packaging is filled with words such as, "More advanced than meditation!!" and "New vibrational energy is total mind time feelings of all shapes and colors united in sound vibrations against all odds," which I think is excellent and right on par with what the album offers. This is an album for journeys or at home listening or anywhere that will allow your mind to wander in the soundscapes. Prism of Eternal Now is 75% ambient and 25% rock and 100% wonderful. Or, as the back also claims, "100% Wide-Spectrum Stereophonic Imaging and a mixture of High Technology and old-fashioned know-how." I highly recommend letting this album be the soundtrack for this period of your life.
