Everything, absolutely everything that been recorded with name Monolake is an unconditional gold, this is no exception. did not get into it straight away, I thought it was too ambientish for monolake but I was so wrong. My current favorite electronic artist, dark and environmental also very varied, mesmerizing most of the time but 'Internal Clock' is another level of mesmerization, favorite part of Monolake is that even thought it is rough beat electronica it never feels dry, there is always depth to be discovered....  
  
p.s. this is the type of music that benefits from quality so if you are intending listening this on laptop speakers just skip it.
