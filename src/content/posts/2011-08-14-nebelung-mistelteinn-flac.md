Simple, relaxing and inspired folk by two men from Germany. I'm nowhere close to being fluent in German, but I'm pretty sure the title means 'Mistletoe'. The music's genre lies somewhere between Contemporary Folk and Neofolk, but rateyourmusic.com describes it as Dark Folk and Neofolk. The vocals, while not excellent, are some of the most relaxing German ones I've heard. He does get better, as can be heard in Nebelung's track on Whom the Moon A Nightsong Sings. I'll post their album 'Vigil' soon...
