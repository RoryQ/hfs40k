I'm not an expert on ambient music, but this album is just something else. Got it because I liked what this man did on the [Pop Ambient 2010](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010161947/http://holyfuckingshit40000.blogspot.com/2010/01/various-artists-pop-ambient-2010-flac.html). Fantastic Ambient that reminds me of some of the best of the genre. And If that wasn't enough, comes with a second disc with the same album remixed which even if it sounds like it's filler, it's really worth your time.
