Blumenkraft (or "Flower Power" in German) is the definition of ethereal harmonic perfection. A blissful mix of ambient/chillout/dub/psychedelic/reggae. Blumenkraft has quite a diverse feel. With its rich bass, rhythmic melodies, and at times haunting vocals, Ott pulls you outta the club and out for a ride to the African plane, the islands of the Caribbean and to the misty forests of Asia. Totally awesome and extremely listenable: in the car, chilling out, as background tunes, or to go to sleep. Everyone who has heard me play this cant help but groove to it. If you know what ails you, buy this album.
