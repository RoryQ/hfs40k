[![](/holyfuckingshit40000/images/covers/2009-06-15-wayne-shorter-speak-no-evil-flac.webp)](/holyfuckingshit40000/images/covers/2009-06-15-wayne-shorter-speak-no-evil-flac.webp)One of the leaders in bringing the avant-garde to Blue Note in the 60s, Wayne Shorter is a quintessential musician's musician: as excellent a composer as he is an improviser. This session, consisting completely of Shorter originals, boasts some amazing ideas. Filled with minor keys, haunting melodies, and subversive rhythms, Shorter pulled out all of the stops, so to speak, on this one. Another aspect of the success of this date can be attributed to Shorter's supporting cast: Freddie Hubbard, Herbie Hancock, Ron Carter, and Elvin Jones. If that isn't enough to tell you that this must be a classic recording, I have no clue what is.
