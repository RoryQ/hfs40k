Amazing review :  
Godspeed You Black Emperor are en route to a concert in Amsterdam when their plane crashes in a remote part of Africa. Fortunately they all survive but most of their equpiment is mangled, accept for a keyboard and a couple guitars. All of the members suffered brain damage and now their only memory of music is one song: Taphead by Talk Talk. Later, while looking for help they come accross a local tribe. the tribesmen invite them to stay. that night GYBE is awoken by tribal drums and chants. They exit their tents to go witness this event. while watching this, they are given a small bowl filled with a foul smelling brown liquid. A tribesmen keeps repeating the word Ayahuasca, Ayahuasca!! he then makes a motion of raising his hands to his mouth, indicating they are to drink the potion. the band decides to drink it. time passes and eventually the ayahuasca has reached their brains and they are experiancing an out of this world psychedelic experiance. They can feel the tribal drums inside of their soul. They quietly get up and go to get their keyboard and guitars and the one sole remaining amp that wasnt destroyed.  
  
This album is a recording of what happened that night.
