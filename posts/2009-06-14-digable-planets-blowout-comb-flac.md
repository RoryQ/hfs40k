Even though it's overshadowed in the popular eye by its predecessor, from which the Grammy Award winning single "Rebirth of Slick (Cool Like Dat)" came, to the seasoned hip-hop connoisseur, this is obviously the better album. From the incorporation of live instruments to the increased social awareness and literacy of their rhymes, this album is overall an excellent tightening of the trademark jazzy Digable Planets sound. A must listen.
