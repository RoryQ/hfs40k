Jeffrey Lee Pierce is one of my favourite vocalists in rock music. A lot of people think that 'Fire of Love' is the better album, but this is my favourite, if only because his voice is better on tracks like 'Carry Home', 'Like Calling Up Thunder', 'Brother and Sister' and 'Mother of Earth'. Also, there are less psychobilly songs which is good (even though the band probably preferred the psychobilly ones, because over half the album is in that style)
