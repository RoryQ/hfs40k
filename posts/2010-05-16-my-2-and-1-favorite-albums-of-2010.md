I'm not going to say the album/artists names for them in the regular way, because I noticed that these albums get taken down a lot. So, the artist and album name will be in reverse order for each word. The first album, is a cool jazz album, and is definitely one of the best modern jazz albums I have ever heard. This one is available in FLAC. My favorite album of the year, is unfortunately not in FLAC or even 320kbps. It goes at a range between 216-240kbps. However, I find this album to be so captivating. I like to think of it like NSB, except instead of having drone songs and then folk, this album has drone songs and then black metal/noise songs with psychedelic elements. Enjoy!
