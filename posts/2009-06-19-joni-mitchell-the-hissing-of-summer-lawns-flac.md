Even more jazz-inflected than the last, this album was too experimental for much of the audience she gained on Court and Spark to take. Those who listened, however, were bound to be floored by the increasing complexity of her arrangements and way her voice deftly navigated them. The in-depth character studies that had come to be her trademark are sprinkled with social and political commentary (something that can also be seen on Court and Spark). In other words, another stellar album.
