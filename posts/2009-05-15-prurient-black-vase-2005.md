If the thought of hundreds of needles repeatedly stabbing you in your eardrums, some digging in deeper than others, is appealing to you - chances are you're going to like this album. It has a cohesiveness and ability to completely suck you in that is rarely met within the genre. Focused, unrelenting, and recommended.
