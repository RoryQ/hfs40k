Other than that, reasons to love this release include the playing of tenor man George Adams and pianist Don Pullen, whose free-but-not-quite styles of playing are excellently suited to the "Mingus sound". You'll find here the complex and intricately layered compositions and soloing that you've come to expect from a Mingus record. Frankly, there's not much more that I need to say. Grab this.
