Thought they were interesting, so I picked up a CD in support. I've listened through about 2 or 3 times and what I've heard is pretty nice. Vocalist's voice intentionally oscillates between frequencies a lot (warbles), and it's definitely the most salient feature of their sound. The band have this style of rock that's well-suited to the many rainy days here in Gainesville (mainly because they're from here). Occasional trumpet flourises, pedal-steel, multiple intertwining guitar lines, cello parts, etc. Very "indie", but not in a derivative way. 8.1/10 from Pitchfork for what it matters.
