More funk-ridden than its predecessor (meaning more fluent and less fractured), though it still is miiiiiiiiles away from a Johnny Pate session. How Much Longer is relatively calm in the first half but gets damn ugly/wonderful in the middle, these guys had a knack for throwing sound/noises/harmonies so that everything came crashing towards the listener.  
The recording is a bit more muffled than on Y, but the content is again as obvious as possible, with violent funky numbers like Blind Faith, Feed The Hungry and Rob A Bank. They also sound more playful and a bit more ”conventionally” jammy on this recording, even if the message of anger stayed the same. Their studio albums are essential listening for those into this, I strongly believe in their historical importance. Open yo ears!
