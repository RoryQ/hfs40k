And what a way to end! *The Visitor* is one of the best recent musical releases I can think of. A completely instrumental affair, this effort finds O'Rourke moving on, without the biting lyrics that we'd grown accustomed to over the past few releases, again bringing the focus to his knack for wonderful arrangements a la *Bad Timing*, but this time raising the ante many fold. Consisting of a single, 38 minute track, *The Visitor* plays like a revolving door of music; instruments dart in and out -- a glockenspiel here, a piano there, guitar everywhere -- while melodies appear and disappear, all the while maintaining a consistent and logical progression that is simply a *joy* to behold. Analyze it. Sleep to it. Listen to it. *Lov*e it.
