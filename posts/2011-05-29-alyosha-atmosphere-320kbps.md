hello,  
  
i am alyosha, a friend of tolkkii's. he enjoyed this release and suggested i post it on this blog.  
  
 I have  been producing beats/music for about 6-7 months now. i started out messing around with virtual DJ a long time ago just making small remixes and interesting loops, and moved on to a sequencer + keyboard to make beats and experiment under different aliases doing electronic music and synthpop. this alias, alyosha, is what i use for all my hip hop beat projects. now to explain this release:  
  
i worked for about two weeks on this, and i mainly made it for myself. the back story is that i wanted something to listen to at work that was really chill/mood music type shit and i've had a really hard time finding things similar to vintage volume 2 by jay dee (j dilla) which has always been my go-to album for that type of music. so i decided to make my own album that would work in that same regard, atmospheric, chill beats that work in most settings. i figured i'd share it since i was actually really pleased with the results and thought others would like this. if you're a fan of old jay dee, or even the more chill side of pete rock,  i'd say it is definitely worth checking out; or if you just need music to relax to.  
  
  
it isn't all samples, either. i play the keys on alone in the city, good morning, being joe, and the various small key parts on slow nights, depression. i programmed all the drums myself, strings, synth pads, etc all that good stuff.  
  
sample sources, for those who are curious:  
  
the piano sample in father  is a thelonius monk song off alone in san francisco, i'll edit this when i remember which one  
  
  
the vocal sample in alone in the city is marvin gaye's "to keep you satisfied"  
wes as the title implies is wes montgomery's polka dots and moonbeams even if it really sounds nothing like it  
  
highway revisited has a piano sample from a shin megami tensei nocturne song. also can't remember which one i made the rough copy of this song a month ago and only recently revised it.  
  
slow nights samples "good morning,captain" by slint  
  
depression samples katy song by the red house painters  
  
less is a mixture of one lick less and a guitar part from demon sings love songs by unwound.  
  
childhood samples "heavy water/rather be sleeping" by grouper
