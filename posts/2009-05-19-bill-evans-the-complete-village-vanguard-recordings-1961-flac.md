Pluses include better transitions between the songs, as spoken introductions are included, as well as increased ambient noise (the idle banter of the patrons and the clinking of glasses) that really add to the 'live' atmosphere, heightening the impact of the already great music. Words can't really describe how magical the interplay between Evans' piano and LaFaro's bass are, so I'll let the music do the speaking for itself. One of my favorites.
