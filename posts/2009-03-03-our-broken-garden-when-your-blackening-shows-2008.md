Country: Denmark  
Genre: Dream Pop  
  
"This album is anchored in much the same theme as Lost Sailor, its nautical symbology and slow, lilting rhythm giving a lingering impression of floating on the very calmest of waters, buoyed by alluring siren-like vocals. Like a half-remembered dream, delicate melodies drift by in a haze of strings, chimes and electronic effects courtesy of a Hammond organ, the sparse soundscape conveying an overwhelming sense of the isolation it was conceived in. The effect is by turns poignant and profoundly unsettling - especially in the standout title track, which, as its name might suggest, is the darkest of this melancholic collection. The music is a timeless combination of retrospective elements in the modern sound picture."
