I was in a good mood so I bought this to upload in FLAC on my blog and nobody got it. Maybe there was a problem with my upload to megaupload, or maybe nobody trusts Meth after *How High*. Trust me on this one, *Tical* is as essential as anything by Ghostface or Raekwon, and miles above most of the other shit that Wu-Tang members release solo. It's got some of RZA's dirtiest production, fucking heavy drums, grimy ambient soundscapes, and Method Man is on the top of his game, funny, romantic, intensely skilled with the hazy voice you should love him for by now.
