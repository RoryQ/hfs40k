 Suddenly, disregard everything you knew about Autechre.   
  
Whenever Autechre releases a new album, it's kind of a big deal as you can always count on them to have evolved by a leap. The trademark glitchy breakbeats are now either nonexistent or subverted, organic sounds with tons of FM synthesis taking the stage instead. There's the same mechanical precision and complexity you'd expect to find in new Autechre, while the driving forces here are melodies and harmonies reminiscent of their old material.  
  
But really Oversteps goes beyond both eras. It's bound to confuse, divide and eventually impress its listeners.
