Quite honestly one of our most anticipated albums of the year so far, and a genuine contender for the best of 2010, 'Does It Look Like I'm Here?' is the 12th LP from the based synth dreamer, Emeralds.  
  
This is an essential purchase for lovers of Fever Ray, Popol Vuh, Klaus Schulze, Autechre, Aphex Twin or Jan Hammer. Brilliant.
