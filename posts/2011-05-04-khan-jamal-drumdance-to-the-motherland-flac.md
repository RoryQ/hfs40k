Thanks Khan Jamal and your insanely obscure album for answering my question. This shit is amazing. A fucking trip, man. Reverb on the reverb on the reverb. I'm listening to it while I'm writing this and can feel myself getting more incoherent and run-on-prone because my mind is being attuned to and put into contact with the cosmic echoes of a common universal past present and future man it's just bending my lobes of gray matter like a straw bro now it's time for eggs and milk and Khan Jamal dude just whoa
