If you haven't heard "Unity", you're missing out on a top 50 jazz album all-time. But "Lawrence of Newark" is no slouch either. Young continues with his extremely unique method of playing the organ in an avant-garde jazz setting (it's usually used more in a soul jazz role). James "Blood" Ulmer, a guitarist you'll be hearing more of soon, also partakes in the proceedings. There are a ton of different instrumentalists so I won't bother to name them all. Just listen.
