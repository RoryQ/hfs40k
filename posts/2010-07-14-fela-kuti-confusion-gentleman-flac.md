In case you guys don't know, it's the country that I hail from, and Fela Kuti is the national musical hero. His influence and fanbase extends to quite a bit of the other countries in West Africa as well, and I can imagine that Ghanaians were jamming to this on their amazing World Cup run to the Final Four (fuck you Uruguay, Ghana won that game).

Anyway, the star of the show here is the track *Confusion*, an EPIC, 25-minute long slab of avant-garde FUNK. Avant-garde because the first 5 minutes is one of the best intros to a song every, and when the song's jam starts up, it just escalates from there. The lyrics are about confusion in urban Lagos due to the many languages spoken, as well as other varied social topics. Saxophone solos, trumpet solos, guitar freakouts, and "WHEN WE TALK SAY CONFUSION, EVERYTIN' OUT OF CONTROL". Listen to this, *please.*
