I mean, the instrumental chops on this album are just out of this world. The drummer fits into the pocket like a glove, and the bassist follows. The Meters are from the Big Easy (New Orleans), so you KNOW they know how to get down. For any of you hip-hop heads out there, check *Just Kissed My Baby* and compare it to Public Enemy's *Timebomb* from their first album. Funky.
