[![](https://web.archive.org/web/20091028113157im_/http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_JdyrURA7JYI/StzpU8lUf8I/AAAAAAAAAJg/M8wGBqahTdc/s400/aylerwich.jpg)](https://web.archive.org/web/20091028113157/http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_JdyrURA7JYI/StzpU8lUf8I/AAAAAAAAAJg/M8wGBqahTdc/s1600-h/aylerwich.jpg)Gosh darn it, this may be some of the best free jazz I've ever heard. Ayler brothers Albert and Donald play tenor sax and trumpet and bounce off each other with marching melodies and chaotic improvising. Also, there's a white guy wanking on a cello and the drummer and dual bassists are nothing short of amazing. This is probably my favorite compilation of all time, and the first disc is a masterpiece (the second album is pretty close to perfection as well.)
