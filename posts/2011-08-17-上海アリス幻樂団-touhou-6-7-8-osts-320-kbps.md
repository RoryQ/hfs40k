Touhou, really? Oh man, that guy's such a weeaboo. I bet he's fat, hasn't left his house in years, and he's got a room full of anime figurines and posters. Just so you know, I'm only in it for the music and the hentai.  
  
So the Touhou Project is a series of video games (mostly bullet hell shooters) with rather fanatic followers. One guy known as ZUN seems to be behind everything. If nothing else has come out of it, at least the music's good. Really good, and you don't even need to know or care about Touhou to like it. The trademark style often leans toward an energetic (J-)pop aesthetic, but it's certainly more technical and abstract than that, with colorful orchestral instrumentation, oriential spices, and no high-pitched lyrics about how a girl left a guy. These three soundtracks are often considered the best, most remixes being based on them. Indeed, there's a terabyte's worth of direct Touhou rearrangements and "spiritual remixes" out there, but nothing beats the originals. I should know since I've listened to all of it.  
  
Mind you, these are fun to learn on the piano too. If there's a track you want to play, someone's already made sheet music of it.
