Okey so here's the latest album by a longrunning, kindof a legend in electronic music imo. You may know him from being the other half of "Gridlock" and I think this album has a bit of similarities to the music they made back then, though it's not really the same at all. There's a bit of this shoegazeish post-rock ambient hybrid around as well, which is the kind of stuff he has been making under "Bitcrush", (who released recently a new album too which is pretty awesome, worth checking out). As a fan of "emotional electronic" music myself I find this album to be extremely good. In fact so good I would say it's the best of 2010 so far, for me. I'm sharing it now with all of you because you probably really should check this out if you are into electronic music at all. It's really cool how atmospheric and beautiful it is with the harsh rhythmic noise beats.  
  

If you, like me, happen to end up loving the album. You can buy it from the label's website [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20111010175344/http://n5md.com/discography/181/Ventricle), for a rather fair price.
