After the critical praise they received from Duck Stab, The Residents felt like they had done something wrong. They recorded Eskimo to correct this. Eskimo is a full tribal experience recorded using only their normal rock instruments. You wouldn't know it unless I told you.

Unfortunately for The Residents, Eskimo also received positive critical reception. This time it was praised for being original and unique. Go figure.
