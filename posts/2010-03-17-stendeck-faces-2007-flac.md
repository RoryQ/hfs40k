I posted this in here a very long time ago, but sadly that FLAC had one corrupted song which was really annoying. Here's this godly album again. Faces is really expressive and emotional / melancholic noisy IDM/Ambient album that about everyone should hear. Rather than being overly complex mathematic IDM "wankery" it's really atmospheric and builds up like a post-rock album. I love it. Simply put nothing less than my favorite album ever.
