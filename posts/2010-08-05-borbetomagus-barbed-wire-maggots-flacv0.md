Well eff that shite. Borbetomagus (translating to "City of Worms") is a trio of two saxophonists (Jim Sauter, Don Dietrich) and a guitarist/noiser (Donald Miller). They formed in 1979 and became one of the pioneers of the New York free improv/noise scene, setting the standard for other harsh avant-garde acts. Barbed Wire Maggots is a 42-minute long improvisation cut into 2 parts; one on each side of the LP. If you ever wonder where Zs got the influence for their newest album, listen to this.
