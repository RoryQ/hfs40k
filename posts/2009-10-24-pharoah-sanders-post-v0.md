Famous tenor saxophonist who followed along the lines of Coltrane's later period. All of these albums have long, side-length compositions. Deaf Dumb Blind has a strong afrobeat influence. Karma, his most famous, includes the famous piece "The Creator Has a Master Plan" with guest vocals by Leon Thomas, flute, and loads of cool percussion. On Jewels of Thought, Side A is mostly a duet between Thomas and Sanders. While Side B has Sanders used other reeds, like the bass clarinet. Black Unity is a mix of all of these influences into one big piece.
