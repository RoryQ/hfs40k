Each one of these pieces is an original composition, and the large ensemble (almost 20 instrumentalists) allow them to be fleshed out with layers upon layers of depth that wouldn't be possible in a normal quartet or quintet setting. The flute solo on "Faded Beauty" is not to be taken lightly, and I especially enjoy the presence of a tuba in the ensemble. An excellent listen that's had me continuously coming back for more.
