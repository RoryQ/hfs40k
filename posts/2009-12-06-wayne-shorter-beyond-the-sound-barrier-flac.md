Done in essentially the same vein as Alegría, which preceded it by 4 years, Beyond the Sound Barrier is a lesson in expertly and artfully done deconstruction (and re-construction, because too many times music is simply left in piles of rubble by overzealous avant-gardists). From the opening (wonderfully recorded, I might add; this is a joy to hear in FLAC with good headphones) bass pluckings, to the well-used string section in the arrangement of Felix Mendelssohn's "On Wings of Song", and through to the final fade of applause, this is another excellent outing by Shorter.
