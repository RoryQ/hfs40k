"Ecstasy of Gold" belongs up there not only as one of the most excellent pieces of music composed for a movie, but also as one of the most excellent pieces of music period. The opening piano, the perfectly implemented vocal work, the stirring (pseudo) climax with the percussion rattling in, and then...quiet. And then, it builds up again, to an event better *actual* climax. If you only download this for "Ecstasy of Gold" and "The Trio (Main Title)", you can't be disappointed. At all.
