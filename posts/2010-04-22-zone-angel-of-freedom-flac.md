As soon as I first heard this album, I knew that it was going to be among one of my favorites. The thing that makes this album special is the variety of it. It's not like bands like Mr. bungle where genres change amongst songs, but each song will have its own genre. To me, each song felt so different from the last one, that it sounded like each song was made a different band, and that this was some sort of "various artists" compilation. Each song was also very enjoyable (especially the title track, maybe one of my favorites songs), and in the end even if every song was different, it all worked together as an album. Enjoy!
