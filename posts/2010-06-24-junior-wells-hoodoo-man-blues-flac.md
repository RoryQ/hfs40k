Strangers review :  
I'm not sure it's possible to overrate this album. Probably the best "modern" electronic blues album I've ever heard. This is really Chicago blues at its finest, gritty yet smooth at the same time. Definitely an album to chill out too. Some songs are closer to upbeat r&b than straight blues, which makes it a fine addition to either any blues OR r&b collection. The sound quality as well, for 1965, is unsurpassed, equal with the quality of the music. It's an album I expected to like, but ended up loving.
