"Perhaps the most appropriate album title ever? *Heavy Rocks* is heavy, and good lord does it rock. I've always found that Boris are at their best when they keep it relatively simple, focusing on red-raw riffage and speaker-destroying bass, delivering explosive rock music better than pretty much anyone else on the planet. That's exactly what you get from this album - chunky guitar riffs so crushingly massive they sound like they could level buildings, maniacal, rip-roaring percussion and layer upon layer of distortion, all topped off with some truly epic, visceral singing that kicks the adrenaline up to a whole new level. Opening track "Heavy Friends" is an absolute killer, alternating between graveyard-bass and gleeful meedly-mees for a showcase of sheer attitude, and it's followed by the one-two punch of "Korosu" and "Dyna-Soar", easily two of the best rapid-fire rockers you'll ever hear. The barrage never really lets up after that - barring the gorgeously soulful instrumental interlude "Soft Edge" - making *Heavy Rocks* an incredibly consistent joyride from beginning to end. Boris are the kings of larger-than-life rock bliss, and this might well be their finest hour"
