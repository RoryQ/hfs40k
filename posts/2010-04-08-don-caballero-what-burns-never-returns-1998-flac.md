"When I first heard this album, I didn't get it. Then suddenly about halfway through the third track I realized why, it was the first time I had ever heard the drums played as the lead instrument. After this minor epiphany, I quickly started the disc over again. One of the better ideas I've ever had. Now instead of hearing random guitar noise and crazy timing changes, it all made sense. With the two guitars and the bass providing a good (but ever changing) foundation, the drums are free to do what any four armed man can do with them (I've seen them play live twice, and I'm still convinced he's hiding an extra two to three arms somewhere)."
