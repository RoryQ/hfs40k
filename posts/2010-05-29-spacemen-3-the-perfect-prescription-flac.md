Do you like long periods of sustained feedback? Do you like a heady drone? Do you like to enhance your high? Do you want to know what heroin is like without taking it? Yep they're that good! This is a heavily drug influenced record - "In 1986, all I want to do is get stoned. All I want for you to do is get yourself a little higher." What more needs to be said" Take this gem to the counter of your local record store and say its been prescribed to you - I guarantee you'll enjoy it.
