It's not only Jazz. It's great Jazz. But it's not only great Jazz. It's great Jazz with a piano. But it's not only great Jazz with a piano. It's great Jazz with a piano and a violin. The one operated by Oscar Peterson, the other by Stéphane Grappelli.  
Played to accompany a series of silent movies for a film festival in Paris in 1973 it has been recorded with Niels-Henning Ørsted Pedersen on double bass and Kenny Clarke on drums.  
The artwork included is from the 2007 reissue for the "Jazz in Paris" collection.
