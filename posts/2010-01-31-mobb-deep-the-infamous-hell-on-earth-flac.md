Real gangster rap here. Nothing really tops the dark gritty beats on these two albums. If you haven't listened to these guys before I recommend checking out the infamous first and then Hell on Earth if you want more like that. I think it's a little easier to get in to, though Hell on Earth is great in it's own right.
