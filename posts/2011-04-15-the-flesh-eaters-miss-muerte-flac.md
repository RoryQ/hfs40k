Atavistic / 2004  
  
If you're a fan of hardcore classics like *No Questions Asked* and *A Minute to Pray, a Second to Die*, then you'll find much to like on this, the most recent album by L.A. hardcore legends The Flesh Eaters. It's worth a download just to hear Julie Christensen and Chris D. do the Nancy and Lee thing. All of which reminds me: when are we going to finally see *Forever Came Today* in digital form?  Enjoy!
