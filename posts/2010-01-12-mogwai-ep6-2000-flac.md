Somebody complained that there were too many "soft albums" on the blog recently, so in the interest of keeping everyone happy here's the best post-rock EP that isn't by Godspeed You! Black Emperor. EP+6 brings together all three of Mogwai's previous EPs: 1997's 4 Satin, 1998's No Education = No Future (Fuck the Curfew), and 1999's EP, in a remastered and enhanced CD. So I guess it's actually three EP's rolled into one. And it's FLAC instead of ALAC just for Obi. <3
