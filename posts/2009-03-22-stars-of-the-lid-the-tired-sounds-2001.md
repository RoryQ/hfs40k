"Minimal? Ambient? Not for me; *The Tired Sounds Of* is just too enveloping to be something you just throw on in the background, and way too intense to be considered minimal. Given your full attention at high volume, this opens up like a fissure in the Earth‘s surface. It replaces the air around you, offering you no choice but to keep going forward into this ever-moving, breathing flat of sound. There are details in the depths of these tracks that I doubt even exist; I probably just imagined them, or they were going on in the background once and have become forever entwined with the way I perceive the album. In truth, the country could be flooded when listening, and I’d only notice when the music started speeding up, Sonic style, as I eagerly sprint in search of a bubble to save me. Violins, pianos, flutes and guitars, all separated and exploded within this record, never to be seen again."
